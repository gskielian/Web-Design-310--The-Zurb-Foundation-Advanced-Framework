LEDs and Flashlights

Written by Gregory Kielian on Tue Apr 8 21:39:11 PDT 2014

Create your own flashlight using pro-boards.

The boards have an adhesive on the back -- but be careful as it is really sticky. Each session you will be able to take another board home, so choose your project carefully.

Notice how the LED has a longer end and a shorter end. Grab a battery, and connect the longer end to the shiny side, and the shorter end to the textured side.
